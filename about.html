ABOUT SKYBAN ROOFING SYSTEMS

Excellence in Roofing since 2006. We provide South India with the most durable and stylish roofing solutions available.

OUR JOURNEY

Founded in 2006, Skyban Roofing Systems began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners in Kerala with roofing that can withstand the intense tropical monsoons while maintaining a premium aesthetic. Over the last 19 years, we have grown into a leading provider of Stone Coated Steel Tiles and UPVC Raingutter systems across South India.

Our products are engineered for the extreme. From the heat of the summer to the torrential rains of the monsoon, Skyban roofs are proven to last a lifetime.
